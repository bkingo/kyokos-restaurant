About

At Kyoko's, the richness of imported Japanese wood and the sleek contemporary decor have the same stylish edge as the food. Step inside and settle into our state-of-the-art 20-seat sushi bar or sit in a cozy modernized tatami-style booth for one of the most extraordinary dining experiences in South Florida.

Our Specialities

Ramen

Wheat noodles served in a meat or fish-based broth.

Tempura

Seafood and/or vegetables that have been battered and deep fried.

Gyoza

Flour-based wrappers filled with a piquant mixture of ground pork, garlic chives, ginger, cabbage, and minced garlic..

Takoyaki

Ball-shaped dumplings filled with minced octopus, tempura scrapps pickled ginger and green onion.
